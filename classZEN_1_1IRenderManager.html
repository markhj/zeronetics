Detailed Description

A Render Manager is a way for "something" and a renderer to communicate, without introducing coupling between that "something" and the renderer. The "something" is typically some sort of scene-related object, but it doesn't have to be.

They have different authorities over the render manager, and can both update its data. For instance, a scene-like object can place requests, and the renderer can remove them, when they are processed.
