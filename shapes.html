â­• Custom shapes

You can build your own shapes by extending ZEN::Shape3D, and implementing the ZEN::IShape::make method.

The normal process is defining a constructor which takes the properties you need. A sphere, for instance, needs a radius. But exactly which arguments you have to take depend on the shape you want to construct.

Note
Important: You must implement the make method such that the center of the shape is at (0, 0, 0), otherwise positioning will not work as expected.
